Charities & Donations The Company supports a range of charities which link to its objective of supporting young people through education and skills, particularly in STEM areas. The Company also upholds its connection to the City, seen in its support of City-based charities and educational establishments. Donations All appeals are considered carefully by the Education and Charity Committee. The Committee can approve a one-off donation or a longer-term donation, usually over three years. Further information on how to appeal and the giving criteria can be found on the Benevolent Fund Applications page.
