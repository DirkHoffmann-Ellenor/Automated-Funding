Search our site by keyword Enter your search term here… Peter Harrison Foundation Community Fund The Peter Harrison Foundation Community Fund supports local Surrey community and voluntary groups with grants of up to £5,000. Applications to this programme are managed by the Community Foundation for Surrey. Small grants within Surrey The Fund focuses on those areas of need that we support in our other grantmaking, including providing opportunities for children and young people with disabilities, helping disabled and disadvantaged young people through sport and providing education to disadvantaged young people. By working with the Community Foundation for Surrey, we benefit from the Community Foundation’s knowledge of effective Surrey charities to ensure we reach those in need within the county.
