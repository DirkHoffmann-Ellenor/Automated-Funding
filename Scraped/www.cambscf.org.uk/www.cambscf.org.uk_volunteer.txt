Volunteering at Cambridgeshire Community Foundation is an opportunity to make a lasting difference in Cambridgeshire and be part of shaping a better future for the local community. We are keen to build our team by welcoming people who share our vision of happier, healthier, and fairer lives for everyone across the county. Volunteering at the Foundation offers opportunities to learn new skills, build local connections, and understand Cambridgeshire better than ever before. It’s inspiring to be part of the charity’s work towards a better county and to see the success stories of supported community projects that are making a difference for local people.
