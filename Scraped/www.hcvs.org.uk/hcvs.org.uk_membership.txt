Associate membership Our associate membership is free to all voluntary and community sector organisations operating in Hackney Hackney CVS membership is free to all voluntary and community sector organisations operating in Hackney and provides the following benefits: Access to resource area (computers and printing facilities). Up to 3 hours a week of the small meeting room for free (subject to availability). Discounted rates on our room hire. Priority booking to our training sessions and events.
