Give online You can easily make a one-off donation or set up regular giving using the donation form on this page. Send us a cheque Please send a cheque payable to Cambridgeshire Community Foundation to the following address: Hangar One, The Airport Newmarket Road Cambridge CB5 8TG If you are donating to a particular campaign, please write the name e.g. ‘Surviving Winter Appeal’ on your envelope or the cheque reverse. Make a bank transfer Please email info@cambscf.org.uk for our bank details to make a bank transfer or set up a standing order. Boost your donation with Gift Aid If you are you a UK taxpayer, you could boost your donation by 25p for every £1 you donate at no additional cost to you. If you are giving online, please tick the Gift Aid box. If you have donated via cheque or bank transfer, please complete our short Gift Aid declaration form to make your donation go further.
