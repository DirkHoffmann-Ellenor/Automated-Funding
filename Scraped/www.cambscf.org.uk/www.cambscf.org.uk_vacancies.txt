Why work at Cambridgeshire Community Foundation? We strive to continue growing the Foundation, to increase the available funding for the diverse range of causes that we support within our community. To help us achieve this, we are focused on growing our profile, donor development, and strengthening the Foundation’s capacity, systems and processes, to respond flexibly to the ever-changing priorities and needs of our local community. To support the charity’s growth, we are keen to build our team by welcoming people who bring relevant expertise and share our vision of happier, healthier, and fairer lives for everyone across the county. Working at Cambridgeshire Community Foundation is an opportunity to make a lasting difference in Cambridgeshire and be part of shaping a better future for the local community.
