If you are a Lambeth resident and require advice on student finance/money, the Walcot Advice project based at Centre 70 in West Norwood can help you. To book an appointment, please contact our advisor via email rkaur @centre70.org.uk . This service is free and confidential for Lambeth residents. We have put together information on additional resources (accessing other grants, work experience and general support) to help you make the most of your studies - details here . If you have any further questions or comments on our individual grant giving please email [email protected] for support.
