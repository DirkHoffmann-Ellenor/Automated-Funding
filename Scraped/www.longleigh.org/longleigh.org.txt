We are Longleigh Foundation A grant-making charity working to create real change for social housing residents across the UK. Everyone deserves a safe, secure home and the chance to thrive. But when life is difficult, the right help isn’t always easy to find. That’s why we’re here. ﻿ Longleigh Foundation provides funding, support and research that helps people in social housing move forward with dignity and confidence. Our work includes: Emergency grants to ease financial pressure Specialist programmes supporting emotional and financial wellbeing Research that drives fairer policies and better services We are here to listen, act and fund fairly, with compassion, collaboration and commitment at the core of everything we do.
