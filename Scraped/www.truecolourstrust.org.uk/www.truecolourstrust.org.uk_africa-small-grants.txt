Africa Small Grants True Coloursâ Africa Small Grants programme provides funding to support the development of palliative care across Africa. The programme is administered by the African Palliative Care Association and applications are considered twice each year. Funding is primarily for one-off projects, in particular items which directly improve the patient experience and the standard of palliative care services delivered. Priority is given to the following, in no particular order: Equipment for patients Palliative care for children and young people Palliative care medicines Capital improvement costs Increasing access to palliative care in rural areas Training courses for palliative care service providers held in Africa. For more information about the Small Grants programme and to apply, please visit the Small Grants website . If you have any questions about the programme, please contact David Byaruhanga at APCA who will be happy to support you during the application process.
