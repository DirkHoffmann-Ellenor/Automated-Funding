Home > Apply for funding > Other support Other support available Support in Bristol Voscur is a support and development agency for the Bristol VCSE (Voluntary, Community and Social Enterprise) sector and provides a range of advice and guidance for local groups and organisations. Black South West Network (BSWN) supports Black and racially minoritised community organisations based in Bristol and the wider South West on governance, business planning, funding, communication, and evaluation and monitoring. Support in Bath and North East Somerset (BANES) 3SG is an independent membership network supporting over 250 local charities, social enterprise and community groups across Bath and North East Somerset. It aims to strengthen the Third Sector in B&NES by raising the profile of its members, providing 1:1 support, training and networking. It also advocates for the sector, working at a local, regional and national level. Support in North Somerset Voluntary Action North Somerset (VANS) provides advice and guidance to groups and organisations as well as helping individuals to access local volunteering roles. Support in South Gloucestershire CVS South Gloucestershire provides a range of advice and guidance for local groups and organisations.
