The organisations we have donated to include: Grant-making Data We are working with 360Giving to make data about our grant-making freely accessible, so that funders and charities across the UK can use data to improve grant-making practice. This spreadsheet contains details of all grants awarded by the Foundation from 2010 until July 2024, formatted to the 360Giving data standard. The Foundation will continue to publish new grants via 360Giving at least once a year. This work is licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License . This means the data is freely accessible to anyone to be used and shared as they wish. The data must be attributed to the Tuixen Foundation. Charity Number: 1081124
