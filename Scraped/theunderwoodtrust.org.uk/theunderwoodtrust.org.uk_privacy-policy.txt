Privacy policy Privacy policy How we collect your information The information we hold about you is collected by how you engage with us such as processing your application through our website, via email, the post, over the phone or from discussions with you. We may sometimes use information that is publicly available to gain a better understanding of your charity when assessing grants or donations. The type of information we hold and why we use it The information we hold will include your name, address, telephone number, email address and bank details which are needed to process and pay grants and donations. Will we shall your information with anyone else? We will not sell or swap your information with any agency or other charity. We will not share personal information about you or with third parties without your consent unless permitted to do so by law (for example to government bodies and law enforcement agencies). Storing your information We will hold your personal information on our secured systems for as long as is necessary for the relevant activity (usually a maximum of 6 years) or as long as is set out in any relevant contract you hold with us. We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect.
