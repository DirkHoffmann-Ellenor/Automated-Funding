Find the right fund for you Before you start Please note: We only fund work that is charitable , as defined by the Charity Commission . Generally, our funds take four to six months from application to decision . We are unable to cover costs that have already been incurred. Start the fund finder You don’t need to prepare any paperwork; we’re just going to ask you a few simple questions about: how soon you need funding; in what country the work will take place; what type of work you want to do; the setting for delivery of the work; and who the work will help or support. You will be asked a maximum of five questions. At the end, you’ll be suggested the fund that is the best fit for you. Find out more
