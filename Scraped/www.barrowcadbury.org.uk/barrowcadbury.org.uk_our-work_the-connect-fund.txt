Connect Fund The Connect Fund was a £6.3 million fund that the Barrow Cadbury Trust managed in partnership with Access – the Foundation for Social Investment between 2017-2024. It was set up to strengthen the social investment market to better meet the needs of charities and social enterprises in England. On the link above you’ll find a back catalogue of tools, reports and resources developed by the Connect Fund’s grant partners to share and support learning about the social investment market. Evaluation Read a full evaluation of the Connect Fund 2017-2024 . A summary evaluation report ‘Connect Fund Learning and Evaluation 2027-24’ was published in January 2025.
