Complaints about funded organisations Service related complaints People’s Health Trust works with a broad range of partners to deliver our funding, policy, and research programmes across Great Britain, all of which aim to stop people dying too young and achieve health justice for communities experiencing disadvantage and marginalisation. Since 2011, we have raised over £133m for communities across England, Scotland and Wales. We receive income from partnerships with corporate partners , major donors, direct donations , grants and contracts.
