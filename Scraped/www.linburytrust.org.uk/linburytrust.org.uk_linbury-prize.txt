The Linbury Prize is bi-annual prize given for excellence in stage design, including design for dance, and costume. Highly regarded, The Linbury Prize has supported the careers of talented designers for more than 30 years. The Linbury Prize is delivered in partnership with the National Theatre and you can read more about the Prize, the winners from 2023, and previous winners here: https://www.linburyprize.org.uk/ .
