Eligibility What we won’t fund Please check the exclusions list below and do not apply if your work falls within it. We do not generally fund: Animal welfare Organisations that are not UK charities Activities outside the UK (except the Glendonbrook Foundation) General fundraising appeals Individuals Overseas travel One off events or festivals Advancement of religion Sports and recreation Work which has already taken place NHS hospital trusts Campaigning or lobbying activities Endowments or contributions to other grant making charities Websites, Publications or Seminars Property or vehicle purchase Applications that have been turned down in the previous 12 months Applications that do not fall within our five main areas of focus Grants larger than your organisation’s annual turnover New organisations that do not have a track record of delivery or do not have at least one year’s published accounts available for review
