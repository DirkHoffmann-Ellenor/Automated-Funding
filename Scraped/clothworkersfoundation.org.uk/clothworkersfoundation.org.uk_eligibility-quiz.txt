Your guide to the Open Grants application process We want our application process to be straight forward. Assess Eligibility Read all the guidance on our website (such as the 'Applying for a Grant' page) and take the Eligibility Quiz. Submit Application Eligible organisations will be directed to a page with information on the relevant application form and instructions for beginning the application. Submit this through our online grants portal for review. Review Process Our team will evaluate your application and provide a response within the specified timeframe. We may contact you with questions. Our Decision You will be notified of our decision, along with any next steps. For Large Grants, if you are successful at Stage 1, you will be asked to complete a Stage 2 form before a final decision is made.
