Get Support Grants to charities We are tackling some of society’s most important issues through grants to charities – both local and national – and partnerships with leading organisations in the sector. Our programme is open to charities in England and Wales working with: Early Years (0-5), children affected by domestic abuse, children with special educational needs and disabilities, and people with dementia and their carers. Grants are only made to charities / projects whose beneficiaries are in England and Wales. Take our eligibility quiz and submit an expression of interest.
