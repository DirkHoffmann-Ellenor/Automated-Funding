COMMUNITY ENVIRONMENT Friday 24th September Environmental Funders Network Podcast: Toxics & Pollution “It has been the mantra of our family trust that every human being has the right to good health. Chemical pollution is undermining our health and the very fabric of our environment”. You can listen to our Trustee, Devika Waney Mokhtarzadeh, in conversation with Pesticide Action Network UK , ClientEarth , CHEM Trust and Fidra on the complexities of toxic chemical pollution and its impact on human and environmental health as part of the Environmental Funders Network series ‘Inspiring People’ . This episode : ‘Toxics and Pollution’ was recorded to highlight the vital role of philanthropy in solving environmental and climate issues. As part of Savitri’s work to restore the health of people and planet, we support leading organisations working to strengthen the regulation of harmful chemicals by holding those responsible to account, as well as creating awareness on how to reduce our exposure to these substances.
