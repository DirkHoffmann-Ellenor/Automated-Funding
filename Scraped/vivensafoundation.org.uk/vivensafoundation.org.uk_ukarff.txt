UK Ageing Research Funders’ Forum hosted by the Vivensa Foundation and co-convened by the Vivensa Foundation and the Economic and Social Research Council The UKARFF is a group of funders of academic and clinical research relating to understanding the mechanisms of ageing and ageing-related disease and improving health and social care for older people.
