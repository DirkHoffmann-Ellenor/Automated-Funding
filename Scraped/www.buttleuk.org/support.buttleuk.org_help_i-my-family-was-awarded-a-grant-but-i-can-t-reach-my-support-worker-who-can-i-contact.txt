I / my family was awarded a grant but I can’t reach my support worker, who can I contact? If there are problems with getting in touch with your support worker about your grant, you can contact the agency or service that they work for. If you cannot reach the agency, please call 020 7828 7311 and ask to speak to a member of the Grants Team who will do their best to help you.
