Grants The Trustees of The Lord Leonard and Lady Estelle Wolfson Foundation are pleased to announce that the grants to-date amount to: £14,435,778 of which: £10,119,804 was for medical research into preventative medicine £4,315,974 went into wellbeing Applications for Grants are now closed for 2025/2026 The Foundation is aiming to be the forerunner in assisting preventative medicine and wellbeing in order to support and promote excellence in these fields The Lord Leonard and Lady Estelle Wolfson Foundation is supporting research in UK universities that strengthen the evidence base for wellbeing research in health.
