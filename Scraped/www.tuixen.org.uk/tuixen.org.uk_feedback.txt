Feedback policy The Tuixen Foundation is committed to ensuring that its work is of the highest quality in all areas. The trustees believe that the effective investigation of feedback and learning from this will be an important part of achieving high quality work. The Foundation welcomes suggestions on how its work could be improved and takes these seriously. All feedback to the Foundation will be brought to the attention of the trustee board and will be investigated as appropriate. If you wish to submit feedback, write with details to: The Trustees of the Tuixen Foundation, c/o 440 Strand, London WC2R 0QS. Or email Charity Number: 1081124
